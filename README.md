# 🚗 Cars Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📖 Project Overview

Car dealerships and automotive businesses generate large volumes of sales data every day. However, raw data alone does not provide meaningful insights for business decisions. Without a centralized reporting system, managers often struggle to identify top-performing manufacturers, understand pricing trends, evaluate vehicle specifications, and compare sales performance across different categories.

This project addresses that challenge by transforming raw automotive sales data into an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), explore sales trends, and make informed business decisions through data visualization.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Automotive businesses often face several reporting challenges:

* Sales information is scattered across spreadsheets and databases.
* Comparing manufacturers requires time-consuming manual analysis.
* Pricing trends and vehicle specifications are difficult to monitor.
* Decision-makers lack a centralized view of business performance.
* Generating reports manually increases the risk of errors and delays.

These challenges reduce operational efficiency and make strategic planning more difficult.

---

## 💡 Solution

To solve these problems, I developed an interactive dashboard in **Power BI** that consolidates sales data into a single reporting interface.

The dashboard provides:

* Manufacturer-wise sales comparison
* Average vehicle price
* Average engine size
* Total vehicle mileage
* Interactive filtering by Manufacturer, Fuel Type, Model, and Year of Manufacture

Users can dynamically filter the data and instantly explore different business scenarios without modifying the underlying dataset.

---

## 🔍 Key Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer important business questions such as:

* Which manufacturer generates the highest total sales value?
* How do average vehicle prices differ across manufacturers?
* What is the average engine size of vehicles in the inventory?
* Which fuel types dominate the market?
* How does vehicle pricing change across different models and production years?
* Which filters reveal the most profitable product segments?

---

## 🛠 Analytical Approach

The project follows a complete Business Intelligence workflow:

1. Data Cleaning and Transformation using Power Query.
2. Data Modeling to establish relationships between datasets.
3. Creation of DAX measures for business KPIs.
4. Dashboard design focused on usability and executive reporting.
5. Interactive visualizations for exploratory analysis.

---

## 📊 Business Impact

This dashboard enables business managers and analysts to replace manual reporting with automated, interactive analytics. Instead of spending hours preparing reports, users can quickly identify sales trends, compare manufacturer performance, monitor pricing, and support strategic decisions with reliable data.

By providing a single source of truth, the dashboard improves reporting efficiency, enhances decision-making, and demonstrates how Business Intelligence tools can convert raw sales data into actionable insights.
